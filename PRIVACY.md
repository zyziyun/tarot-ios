# Privacy Policy

**TarotMystica** — Last updated: April 13, 2026

## Overview

TarotMystica is designed with your privacy in mind. Your tarot readings, questions, and personal data stay on your device.

## Data Collection

TarotMystica does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

- **AI Readings**: All AI-powered tarot interpretations are processed entirely on your device using on-device machine learning. No data is sent to any server.
- **Reading Journal**: Your saved readings are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. They are never uploaded or shared.
- **No Accounts**: TarotMystica does not require sign-up or sign-in. There are no user accounts.
- **No Tracking**: We do not use advertising trackers or sell any data.

## Analytics

TarotMystica may use Apple's standard analytics (App Analytics) and Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics such as app launches and feature usage. This data cannot identify you personally and is used solely to improve the app experience.

## Third-Party Services

- **Firebase Analytics** (optional): Collects anonymous usage data. See [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## Data Storage

All user data (readings, journal entries, preferences) is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app removes all data.

## Children's Privacy

TarotMystica does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
