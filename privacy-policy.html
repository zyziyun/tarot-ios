Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 13, 2026

TarotMystica ("we", "our", "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

1. Data We Collect

Analytics Data: We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data such as which features are used most often (e.g., spread selection, AI interpretation completion rate). This data is aggregated and cannot identify you personally.

Crash Reports: We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymous crash reports to help us fix bugs and improve stability.

Local Data: Your tarot reading journal entries are stored only on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not have access to this data.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any personal identification information.
  • We do not collect your location data.
  • We do not require account creation or login.
  • We do not sell or share any data with third parties for advertising purposes.

3. AI Interpretation

When you use the AI tarot interpretation feature with a cloud API provider, your question and card data are sent to the configured API endpoint to generate a reading. This data is not stored on our servers. When using a local on-device model, all processing happens entirely on your device.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics (Google) — Privacy Policy

5. Data Retention

Analytics data is retained according to Firebase's default retention policies. On-device journal data persists until you delete the app.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: tarotmystica@outlook.com
