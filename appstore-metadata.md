## English Description

Discover what the cards have to say. TarotMystica brings the ancient art of tarot to your fingertips with beautiful hand-drawn cards and AI-powered interpretations.

FEATURES

- 78 beautifully illustrated tarot cards (Major & Minor Arcana)
- 17 unique spreads from classic Three-Card to advanced Celtic Cross
- AI-powered tarot interpretations that understand your question
- AI spread recommendation based on your question
- Follow-up conversations with AI for deeper insight
- On-device local AI model support for complete privacy
- Daily Card widget for your home screen
- Reading journal to save and revisit past readings
- 4 interpretation styles: Concise, Standard, Poetic, Analytical
- Supports English, Chinese, French, and Spanish
- Dark, mystical design crafted for the tarot experience

Whether you seek guidance on love, career, health, or personal growth, TarotMystica provides thoughtful, personalized readings powered by advanced AI. Your readings stay on your device — your journey is yours alone.
